# Feeding Our Future Defendant Roster

**Total charged:** 78 (per DOJ press release November 24, 2025)
**Total convicted:** 65 (per DOJ press release April 9, 2026)
**Initial wave:** 47 defendants charged September 20, 2022 (DOJ-OPA archive)
**By March 2023:** 60 defendants charged (Star Tribune compiled list)
**By February 2024:** 70 defendants charged (KSTP)
**By November 2025:** 78 defendants charged (latest DOJ release)

Total scheme amount: **$300M+** (per Comer's March 4, 2026 opening statement at Walz/Ellison hearing).

Source: Star Tribune compiled list ([https://www2.startribune.com/defendants-charged-feeding-our-future-fraud-2022-federal-prosecutors-meals-usda/600258471/](https://www2.startribune.com/defendants-charged-feeding-our-future-fraud-2022-federal-prosecutors-meals-usda/600258471/)) for #1-60 + DOJ press releases for #61-78.

Ages are from time of charges per Star Tribune.

## Convicted Kingpins

- **#48. Salim Ahmed Said, 33** — Empire Cuisine (Shakopee, MN). Convicted on all counts March 19, 2025. Feds seek $7.8M forfeiture as "Worst of the Worst." Sentencing pending.
- **#50. Aimee Marie Bock, 41** — Founder of Feeding Our Future. Convicted on all counts March 19, 2025. Sentencing pending.

## Recent Sentencings (April 2026)

- **#23. Abdullahe Nur Jesow, 62** (now 65) — Columbia Heights resident, operated Lake Street meal site. Sentenced **April 9, 2026 to 43 months federal prison + $866,458 restitution**.
- **Suleman Yusuf Mohamed** — Star Distribution operator. **Pled guilty April 2, 2026.** Star Distribution received $10.1M in Federal Child Nutrition Program funds.
- **Gandi Yusuf Mohammed** — co-defendant brother. Pled guilty April 2, 2026. Agreed to $8,662,287 restitution.

## Recent Indictments (Sept 2025 — Feb 2024)

- **Said Ereg + Najmo Ahmed** — Evergreen Grocery and Deli (Minneapolis corner store). Falsely claimed 3,000 meals/day from April 2020 through April 2021. Submitted reimbursement for 1.4M meals. Received $4.2M. Sent $100K kickbacks to FOF.
- **Ikram Yusuf Mohamed + 6 family co-defendants** — recruited family members to open meal sites and a phony food distribution company.
- **Asha Farhan Hassan, 28** — separate EIDBI autism services Medicaid fraud, charged September 2025 (offered kickbacks to families to enroll kids without diagnoses).
