# MIGA Nimba Iron Ore — Board Approval Date and Bethel Tenure (Negative Finding)

## Approval date
The MIGA project page for the Nimba Iron Ore guarantee — `nimba-iron-ore-0` (the Environmental and Social Review Summary) — discloses:

> "Approval Date: February 18, 2021"
> "Guarantee Amount: $135 million"
> "Project Status: Not Active"

Source: https://www.miga.org/project/nimba-iron-ore-0
Local: [archive/miga/miga_nimba-iron-ore-0_esrs.html](https://github.com/barkleesanders/doge-phantom-winery/blob/main/archive/miga/miga_nimba-iron-ore-0_esrs.html)

The MIGA Board approved the $135M guarantee package on **February 18, 2021**, with the Contracts of Guarantee actually issued at $123.3M (the smaller approved figure was the issued amount) on **March 12, 2021** — ~22 days later.

The ESRS verbatim states the Sponsor relationship as: *"Euronimba Limited of Jersey (Euronimba or the Guarantee Holder), a company 100% owned by High Power Exploration Inc. (United States) (HPX or the Sponsor)."* This collapses the BVI sub for narrative simplicity; the Jersey FSC Annual Return for Feb 19, 2020 (one day after the MIGA Board approval, coincidentally) shows HPX Nimba Holdings Inc. (BVI) as the immediate parent. Both descriptions are consistent — HPX is the ultimate beneficial owner.

## Implication for the dossier

A clean negative finding. The MIGA $123.3M guarantee was approved by the Biden-era World Bank Board (the transition occurred Jan 20, 2021, four weeks before approval). The US ED chair was vacant or in transition; the actual votes by the US delegation in February 2021 were cast by career deputies, not by political-appointee Bethel.

This eliminates one possible "revolving door" theory (Bethel approved the deal at MIGA, then advised firms touching it). His departure from the Bank predates the Nimba decision by ~14+ months.

It does NOT eliminate a separate possibility — that Bethel's professional network connected him to the deal via SinoLatin Capital, Franklin Templeton, or his other Latin-America-focused holdings — but no public record I have found documents that connection.
