# Correction + New Findings — Rose Lake / Ivanhoe Atlantic Investigation

**Filed:** May 6, 2026
**Trigger:** Email from Andrew Kerr (Washington Free Beacon) flagging that EVIDENCE.md and rep-omar.vercel.app stated "Rose Lake Capital invested in Ivanhoe Atlantic" without primary-source documentation.
**Verdict:** Kerr was correct. The investment claim was an inference, not documented. This file logs the correction and the new primary records pulled in response.

## 1. The corrected statement of fact

### What was claimed (now retracted as overreach)

EVIDENCE.md row: *"Rose Lake Capital invested in Ivanhoe Atlantic | Per Apple Lamps Research investigation"*

NETWORK.md: *"Rose Lake's only known investment is in Omar's committee jurisdiction (Ivanhoe Atlantic / Guinea / Liberia)"*

### What is actually documented

**Personnel-level overlap, not equity.** Two facts only:
1. Dr. J. Peter Pham appears as a **Director of HPX (now Ivanhoe Atlantic)** on HPX's June 18, 2024 SEC Form D — `archive/sec-form-d/hpx_form-d_2024-06-18.xml`. Promoted to Executive Chairman + Interim CEO Feb 1, 2026.
2. Dr. Pham was listed as an advisor on Rose Lake Capital's website per the Sept 1, 2025 Wayback snapshot (`archive/wayback/web_archive_org__web_20250901131055_https:__www.roselakecap.com.html`). Names were scrubbed Sept-Oct 2025.

**Whether Rose Lake Capital LLC or any Mynett-affiliated entity holds equity in Ivanhoe Atlantic is not documented in any primary record I have located** as of May 6, 2026.

## 3. New publishable findings (the actual story)

### A. $1.56M in registered Ivanhoe Atlantic lobbying — through Yorktown Solutions and Brownstein Hyatt, NOT Rose Lake

Pulled the Senate LDA API directly. **20 filings on the record:**

**Yorktown Solutions** (Daniel Vajdich's firm)
- Lobbyists: Daniel Vajdich (former PSM Senate Foreign Relations Cmte), Brittany Beaulieu (former LA SFRC + LC Senate Finance), Jonathan Gregory
- Engagement: May 19, 2023 → Q4 2025 (active)
- Fee: $120,000/quarter
- Total disclosed: ~$1.08M
- Verbatim issue description (from registration): *"Yorktown Solutions will explain the: geopolitical and geo-economic importance of iron ore for U.S. national interests… strategic significance of HPX's development of the Nimba iron ore deposit in Guinea… need for HPX to have access to rail transportation through Liberia to the country's deep-water ports — as guaranteed by treaty between Guinea and Liberia, the company's agreement with the Liberian government, as well as Liberian Presidential Executive Order 112 — in order to transport the metal to market; and consequences of a failure."*

**Brownstein Hyatt Farber Schreck**
- Lobbyists: Samantha Carl-Yoder, Lauren Diekman
- Engagement: Q4 2023 → Q1 2026
- Fee: $30K (Q4 2023) then $50K/quarter
- Total disclosed: ~$480K
- **Carl-Yoder was promoted from BHFS lobbyist to Ivanhoe Atlantic non-executive director on Feb 17, 2026** — 27 months as registered lobbyist before joining the board. ([archive/lda-filings/lda-api_ivanhoe-atlantic_full.json](../../archive/lda-filings/lda-api_ivanhoe-atlantic_full.json))

### B. Pham's HPX directorship is in SEC primary records (not just press releases)

HPX Form D filed June 18, 2026 (CIK 0001662195) explicitly lists J. Peter Pham as Director — alongside Bronwyn Barnes (Executive Officer + Director), On Yip Patrick Tsang, Kenneth Lau, David Bohem, Doig Bradley, Robin Sandenburgh.

Offering details: **$324.5M total, $193M sold by 6/3/2024 to 14 accredited investors only**, equity, Reg D 506(b). ([archive/sec-form-d/hpx_form-d_2024-06-18.xml](../../archive/sec-form-d/hpx_form-d_2024-06-18.xml))

### C. MIGA $123.3M Nimba political risk guarantee EXPIRED March 11, 2025

Issued March 12, 2021 to HPX directly with 4-year tenor. Project enterprises: SMFG (Guinea) + Euronimba Liberia Ltd (Liberia). When the Liberian House ratified the rail concession on December 11, 2025, **the political risk insurance backstop was no longer in force**. Worth flagging — primary fact not previously published. ([archive/miga/miga_nimba-iron-ore_project-brief_2021.html](../../archive/miga/miga_nimba-iron-ore_project-brief_2021.html))

### D. Mynett's full LLC portfolio has 6+ entities, not just eStCru and Rose Lake

Per OpenCorporates officer search, Mynett is named as governor / executingofficer / member / manager of:

1. **MYNETT GROUP, (THE) LLC** — DC, 4616 15th Street NW Washington (residence). 14 Feb 2014–12 Sep 2019. INACTIVE.
2. **E STREET GROUP LLC** — DC parent, with Hawaii + Louisiana + CA + Oregon branches. Active in HI/LA, inactive elsewhere.
3. **ESTREETCO LLC** — separate from E Street Group. CA parent (28 Oct 2020–16 Jun 2022 INACTIVE), with branches in DC (revoked 2 Sep 2021), Hawaii, Oregon, NY (active, **80 M St SE FL1 Washington DC 20003**), Louisiana, plus an INACTIVE UK Limited (1 Kings Avenue London N21 3NA).
4. **ESTCRU LLC** — California (terminated 4 April 2026), with NM and WV branches.
5. **EST VENTURES LLC** — DC branch 1 May 2022– (INACTIVE). William Hailer named as **beneficial owner**.
6. **ROSE LAKE CAPITAL LLC** — DE parent + revoked DC branch.

### E. 80 M Street SE, Washington DC 20003 is a shared address

The active New York branch of ESTREETCO LLC and the (now-revoked) DC branch of Rose Lake Capital LLC are both registered at this WeWork. Same physical address.

### F. William Hailer is named as beneficial owner of Rose Lake Capital DC branch — first primary-record link

OpenCorporates record sourced from DC corporate filings (EXTUID_4329642 / native ID C00007470247) lists:

> "inactive **William Hailer beneficial owner**, inactive branch **Rose Lake Capital LLC** (District of Columbia (US), 26 Sep 2022– )"

This means DC's qualifying filing for Rose Lake's foreign-LLC registration in 2022 listed Hailer as a 25%+ beneficial owner. First hard primary-record link of Hailer to Rose Lake's ownership structure. Up to now Hailer's stake was only mentioned in news coverage (Fox News Jan 25, 2026 Hailer profile).

### G. Comer's Feb 19, 2026 deadline blew with no production and no follow-up subpoena

Per Fox News / AOL Feb 20, 2026 reporting, Mynett did not produce documents to House Oversight by the Feb 19 deadline. As of May 6, 2026, no public follow-up letter, subpoena, or contempt referral has been filed. Comer's next major Oversight subpoena (Mar 17, 2026) was AG Bondi on Epstein files — not Mynett. The Rose Lake / eStCru thread appears to have stalled at the Committee.

### H. eStCru terminated April 4, 2026 in California

Confirmed via California Secretary of State business registry. Nine days after Omar filed an amended FD listing eStCru at zero net value (Washington Times, Apr 25, 2026). One of the two companies under Comer's microscope is now wound up, making subpoena-level discovery harder.
