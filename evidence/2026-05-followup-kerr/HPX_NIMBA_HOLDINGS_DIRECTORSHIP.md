## What it says

(Quote runs ~one paragraph in the original, condensed here for the row referenced. List continues with additional Friedland entities including Ivanhoe Mines Ltd., Ivanhoe Electric Inc., I-Pulse-related affiliates, etc.)

## Why this matters

1. **First primary-source confirmation that "HPX Nimba Holdings Inc." exists as a real entity.** Until now, the only place it surfaced was the Jersey FSC Euronimba Limited annual return (Feb 19, 2020) which lists it as the 100% shareholder, plus the Mourant-prepared SR ratifications. Now it's also disclosed in a SEC-filed 10-K — public, stable, citable.
2. **Friedland is personally a Director.** Not a passive holding company controlled remotely — he sits on the board. That ties him directly into the corporate-governance chain that runs:
   - HPX Inc. (Delaware, Friedland CEO/Director)
   - **→ HPX Nimba Holdings Inc. (BVI, Friedland Director)** ←
   - → Euronimba Limited (Jersey #49971, 100% owned by HPX Nimba Holdings)
   - → SMFG (Guinea, 85% owned by Euronimba) and Euronimba Liberia Limited (Liberia)
3. **The disclosure was made in connection with the SES SPAC's IPO (Cayman-incorporated, listed on NYSE).** That means HPX Nimba Holdings Inc. was a known entity to SES underwriters, auditors (Marcum LLP), and ultimately to the SEC. The BVI-domiciled subsidiary is not a hidden vehicle; it's a documented part of Friedland's ICC family-office portfolio.
4. **Confirms the BVI domicile + the "Asset holding" classification** — i.e., HPX Nimba Holdings Inc. is the structural pass-through entity between HPX Inc. (operating) and Euronimba Limited (Jersey holdco of the Guinea/Liberia mining concessions). It does not itself conduct business; it holds the Euronimba shares.
5. **Closes a gap in the prior dossier.** Before today, the existence of HPX Nimba Holdings Inc. was inferable only from the Jersey FSC documents. Now the same entity appears in a SEC-filed annual report — usable as primary corroboration.
