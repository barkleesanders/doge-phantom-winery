# J. Peter Pham — Director of HPX Inc. AND Executive Chairman of Ivanhoe Atlantic AND Advisor to Rose Lake Capital

**Compiled:** May 6, 2026
**Significance:** Closes the central evidentiary gap in the Rose Lake / Ivanhoe Atlantic / HPX coverage. Pham is documented in primary public filings as occupying corporate fiduciary positions on **both sides** of the Friedland-mining / Mynett-policy structure — not just an "advisor" or "consultant" relationship, but formal board / executive seats with fiduciary duty.

## Why this matters

The original Free Beacon inquiry by Andrew Kerr asked whether the dossier had hard documentation for the claim that Rose Lake Capital had invested in Ivanhoe Atlantic Inc., or whether the connection was an inference from Pham's overlapping advisory roles.

This finding **resolves that question in a more substantial form than equity-tracing alone**:
- Pham doesn't just advise both firms, he **holds a board director seat at the parent** (HPX Inc.) AND **runs the operating subsidiary as Executive Chairman and Interim CEO** (Ivanhoe Atlantic Inc.) AND **sits on the Guinea concession-holder's board** (SMFG). And he is simultaneously listed as an Advisor to Rose Lake Capital.
- The "Rose Lake invested in Ivanhoe Atlantic" claim turns out to be the wrong question. The real connection is that **the same individual sits on Rose Lake's advisory board and also leads Ivanhoe Atlantic's operations and HPX's parent board** — that's a more direct conflict of interest than a passive equity stake would be.
- **Equity tracing was the wrong frame.** What we have is multi-hatted fiduciary capture: Rose Lake's "advisor" is the actual operating CEO of the company Rose Lake's other principals (Mynett via Yorktown Solutions, Hailer/Hoffman via the BHFS lobbying contract) are paid to lobby on behalf of.

## Pham's career path through this structure

- **2018-2020**: U.S. Special Envoy for the Great Lakes Region of Africa (rank of Ambassador, granted by President Trump)
- **2020-2021**: U.S. Special Envoy for the Sahel Region of Africa (rank of Ambassador)
- **Post-2021**: Distinguished Fellow at Atlantic Council; previously Vice President for Research and Regional Initiatives + Director of Atlantic Council Africa Center
- **2024-06-03**: First sale of HPX Reg D Round; Pham listed as Director of HPX Inc. on the June 18 Form D
- **By Oct 2024**: Listed as Advisor to Rose Lake Capital on its team page
- **By March 2026**: Identified as Executive Chairman + Interim CEO of Ivanhoe Atlantic Inc.; appointed to SMFG board

The progression is: **U.S. government ambassador** → **think tank fellowships** → **board director of mining holding company (HPX)** → **operating CEO of mining subsidiary (Ivanhoe Atlantic)** + **advisor to investor vehicle (Rose Lake)** + **director of Guinea concession company (SMFG)**.
