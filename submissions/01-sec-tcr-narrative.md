## SECTION C — TYPE OF VIOLATION

Primary alleged violations:

1. **Investment Adviser Act §203 (15 USC §80b-3)** — operating as an unregistered investment adviser
   - Rose Lake Capital website previously claimed *"$60 billion AUM"*
   - Rose Lake Capital ToS states: *"All investment offerings are done within exemptions allowed under the SEC"*
   - SEC EDGAR full-text search returns **zero matching filings** for: "Rose Lake Capital", "eStCru", "Timothy Mynett", "William Hailer", "EstVenture", "West Pike Inc"
   - Verifiable via efts.sec.gov full-text query: https://efts.sec.gov/LATEST/search-index?q=%22Rose+Lake+Capital%22

2. **Regulation D / Form D (17 CFR §230.503)** — failure to file Form D for Reg D exempt offerings
   - Self-admitted reliance on SEC exemptions (per ToS quote above) requires Form D filing within 15 days of first sale
   - SEC EDGAR Form D database returns no filings under any of the named entities

3. **Securities Act §17(a) / Exchange Act §10(b) / Rule 10b-5** — material misstatements
   - The "$60 billion AUM" representation on a public website with no SEC registration, no Form D, no audited financials, no custodian disclosure constitutes a potential material misstatement to investors

## SECTION D — DETAILED NARRATIVE

(Investigators may verify every fact below using the public-record dossier at https://github.com/barkleesanders/doge-phantom-winery — every URL is also locally archived in `archive/` to prevent link rot.)

**1. The disclosure-amendment fork.**
On May 14, 2025, **Rep. Ilhan Omar** (MN-05) filed her 2024 House Clerk Financial Disclosure under penalty of perjury (18 USC §1001), reporting jointly held assets in eStCru LLC + Rose Lake Capital LLC at a combined value of up to **$30 million**. On April 23, 2026, in a recorded interview with the *New York Post*, Omar stated the actual value is *"less than $100,000."* On April 20, 2026, she filed an amended disclosure showing assets between $18,000 and $95,000. Either statement creates federal exposure; the magnitude differential (≈300x) is the material fact.

**2. The capital-raising apparatus.**
Rose Lake Capital represented to the public that it managed $60 billion in assets. Its public website included a Terms of Service explicitly invoking SEC exemptions. The site is currently degraded; the team page including Dr. J. Peter Pham as advisor is preserved on web.archive.org. The entity has no Form ADV (the registration form for investment advisers managing >$100M in assets — the threshold for SEC vs. state registration is much lower than $60B).

**3. The investment of record.**
Rose Lake's only publicly-identified portfolio holding is **Ivanhoe Atlantic** — a $1.8 billion iron ore rail project in Guinea and Liberia. Ivanhoe Atlantic's parent (Ivanhoe Mines) is **40% owned by Chinese state-linked entities (CITIC Metal + Zijin Mining)** per a December 9, 2025 letter from Rep. John Moolenaar (Chairman, House Select Committee on the Chinese Communist Party) to Secretary of State Rubio. The letter is preserved at chinaselectcommittee.house.gov.

**4. The advisor conflict.**
**Dr. J. Peter Pham** is identified on the Wayback snapshot of Rose Lake Capital's team page as an advisor. He simultaneously holds: (a) Chairman of HPX Corp (Ivanhoe Atlantic's parent); (b) Non-Executive Chairman of Ivanhoe Atlantic; (c) advisor on Trump's Africa portfolio; and (d) author of a December 9, 2024 op-ed in the *Liberian Daily Observer* publicly pressuring Liberia to ratify the rail concession — published while holding all four positions.

**5. Why this matters to securities investors.**
If Rose Lake Capital solicited capital from US investors based on the public "$60B AUM" representation, those solicitations occurred outside any registered exempt offering and outside any Form ADV regime. Because the entity is Delaware-domiciled and its principal communicated through a website with US TLD reach, Section 5 / Section 17(a) jurisdiction is straightforward.

**6. Related parallel inquiries (for SEC's situational awareness, not predicate).**
- House Oversight Committee (Rep. James Comer) issued a subpoena-class document request to Mynett on the same conduct
- House Ethics Committee referral made April 2026
- House Select Committee on CCP (Rep. John Moolenaar) is engaged on the Ivanhoe Mines 40% Chinese-state ownership angle
- National Legal and Policy Center (NLPC, Counsel Paul Kamenar) filed an OCC complaint Feb 16, 2026 — that complaint triggered the April 20, 2026 amended disclosure
