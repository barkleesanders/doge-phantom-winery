# DOJ Press Release: Federal Jury Finds Feeding Our Future Mastermind and Co-Defendant Guilty in $250 Million Fraud Scheme

**Source:** https://www.justice.gov/usao-mn/pr/federal-jury-finds-feeding-our-future-mastermind-and-co-defendant-guilty-250-million
**Date:** March 19, 2025
**Note:** Original DOJ page is Akamai-blocked from automated archival. Content was retrieved via Exa search aggregator. The original URL still works in a browser; this file documents that the source EXISTS and what it confirmed.

## Confirmed via Exa retrieval

A federal jury convicted **Aimee Bock** (founder of Feeding Our Future) and **Salim Said** (co-conspirator, Empire Cuisine operator) on all counts in the largest pandemic-related fraud prosecution in the United States. The case centered on the misappropriation of federal child nutrition funds intended to feed children during the COVID-19 pandemic. Total alleged losses: approximately $250 million.
