All Plans Include:

  • Professional U.S. Street Address: Enhance your business presence with a real street address at prime mail centers or office buildings.
  • 24/7 Online Access & Unlimited Cloud Storage: Access your mail anytime, anywhere, with unlimited cloud storage for your convenience.
  • Instant Notifications: Receive real-time email alerts for every new mail and package, so you never miss an important delivery.
  • Detailed Content Scans: Get high-quality scans of your mail content, easily downloadable as PDFs for your records.
  • Local Pickup: Enjoy free local mail and package pickup at your subscribed location.
  • AI Mail Summaries: Instantly generate concise summaries of your scanned mail to quickly understand key details. Free for documents up to 20 pages.
  • Unlimited Junk Mail Discarding: Easily discard unwanted junk mail to keep your mailbox clean and organized.
  • Effortless Consolidation & Forwarding: Consolidate multiple items into one shipment and forward your mail or packages seamlessly.
  • Flexible Usage: Perfect for both personal and LLC business use.
  • Convenient Mobile App: Manage your mail on the go with our user-friendly app for both Android and iOS devices.
  • Comprehensive User Management: Add multiple recipients, manage users and departments with ease.
  • Versatile Shipping Options: Choose from multiple shipping carriers and get real-time shipping rates for the best deals.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Stay informed with shipment tracking and delivery updates right at your fingertips.
  • Detailed Sender Indexing: Easily keep track of who is sending you mail with our sender information indexing feature.
  • Efficient Mail Management: Enjoy a searchable and sortable mail management grid to organize your mail effortlessly.
  • Automated Mail Scanning: Benefit from automated inbound mail scanning for quick and easy access to your mail content.
  • Developer API Access: Programmatic access to mail data and automation for custom integrations.
  • Multiple Mailing Addresses: Easily link and manage multiple mailing addresses within one mailbox account, giving you greater flexibility for both personal and business use.
