# NPR: House GOP removes Ilhan Omar from Foreign Affairs Committee

**Source:** https://www.npr.org/2023/02/02/1153472237/ilhan-omar-foreign-affairs-committee-vote-republicans-remove
**Author:** Lexie Schapitl
**Published:** February 2, 2023
**Note:** Direct fetch timed out; content retrieved via Exa.

Key facts:
- House Republicans voted 218-211 along party lines to remove Rep. Ilhan Omar from the Foreign Affairs Committee
- Cited her past comments about Israel
- Resolution introduced by Rep. Max Miller (R-Ohio)
- Omar called the move "purely partisan"

This event is the predicate for Omar's January 31, 2023 launch of the U.S.-Africa Policy Working Group as an alternative platform after losing her Foreign Affairs seat. See `evidence/EVIDENCE.md` Section 8 (Africa subcommittee timing).
